## Pre-Submission Preparation

### Item Hanging Requirements

**Critical Issue:** Not all items can be hung for powder coating using standard methods.

In November 2024, Elite encountered multiple letters that could not be hung using their standard hanging system. Their solution was to stick problematic items to a plate for painting.

**NBNE Response Protocol:**
- When asked about back surface quality, confirm whether backs will be visible in final installation
- For wall-mounted items where backs face the wall, back surface finish is non-critical
- Communicate this clearly to Elite to allow alternative fixing methods

## Technical Considerations

### Items That May Not Hang

Letters and certain shaped components may not be suitable for standard hanging methods. Elite has demonstrated flexibility in finding solutions (mounting to plates), but this requires:

1. **Early communication** about item geometry
2. **Clarity on finish requirements** for all surfaces
3. **Confirmation of non-critical surfaces** where fixing marks are acceptable

### Surface Finish Priorities

**For Wall-Mounted Applications:**
- Front surfaces: Critical — full quality finish required
- Back surfaces (wall-facing): Non-critical — fixing marks acceptable
- This allows Elite to use alternative mounting methods for difficult items

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Delayed Start Due to Missing PO

**Problem:** Work not commenced despite items delivered (experienced November 2024)

**Solution:** 
- Always raise PO immediately after quote acceptance
- Elite specifically requested PO on 14/11/2024 before starting work
- Do not deliver items without confirming PO has been sent

### Items Cannot Be Hung

**Problem:** Letter shapes or item geometry prevents standard hanging (experienced 26/11/2024)

**Solution:**
- Elite will contact NBNE to discuss alternative mounting
- Confirm which surfaces are non-critical (backs of wall-mounted items)
- Allow Elite to mount items to plates for coating
- Accept minor marking on non-visible surfaces
