# Sign for Hairy Lemon

## Summary
This article covers the vinyl recovery project for the Hairy Lemon sign. The client has requested a matte vinyl finish to recover the existing sign. All necessary materials have been received on site. This job involves applying vinyl to an existing sign structure rather than fabricating a new sign from scratch.

## Project Details

**Client:** Jake (Hairy Lemon)
**Job Type:** Sign recovery with vinyl application
**Material Specification:** Matte vinyl
**Status:** Materials received, awaiting production

## Process Steps

### 1. Pre-Production Assessment
- Confirm all materials have been received and match the specification
- Verify the existing sign dimensions and condition
- Ensure the substrate is suitable for matte vinyl application
- Check that matte vinyl stock is available in the required quantity and colour

### 2. Surface Preparation
- Clean the existing sign thoroughly to remove dirt, grease, and contaminants
- Use appropriate cleaning solution (typically isopropyl alcohol or dedicated sign prep solution)
- Allow surface to dry completely before vinyl application
- Inspect for any damage or deterioration that might affect vinyl adhesion
- Address any surface irregularities that could telegraph through the vinyl

### 3. Vinyl Application
- Measure and cut matte vinyl to size, allowing appropriate margins
- Use application tape suited for matte finishes
- Apply vinyl using wet or dry method as appropriate for the sign size
- Work systematically to avoid air bubbles and wrinkles
- Pay particular attention to edges and corners for proper adhesion
- Use squeegee techniques appropriate for matte materials

### 4. Finishing
- Trim excess vinyl carefully
- Seal edges if required by sign type or environmental exposure
- Remove application tape at appropriate angle to avoid lifting vinyl
- Final inspection for bubbles, wrinkles, or lifting edges

### 5. Installation (if required)
- Coordinate installation schedule with client
- Ensure all fixings and mounting hardware are in good condition
- Complete installation as per standard procedures
- Conduct final quality check with client present if possible

## Warnings and Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **Matte Vinyl Considerations:**
- Matte vinyl shows fingerprints and handling marks more readily than gloss vinyl
- Use clean gloves during application to avoid contamination
- Matte finishes are generally more susceptible to scratching during application
- Heat application may affect the matte finish - test first if heat gun use is necessary

⚠️ **Surface Preparation:**
- Inadequate cleaning is the primary cause of vinyl failure on recovery jobs
- Old adhesive residue must be completely removed
- Signs exposed to kitchen environments (likely for a pub/restaurant) may have grease contamination requiring extra attention

⚠️ **Application Issues:**
- Matte vinyl can be less forgiving than gloss when repositioning
- Air release channels may be less effective in matte materials
- Ambient temperature is critical - avoid application in cold conditions

⚠️ **Client Communication:**
- Email communication issues were noted in the correspondence
- Maintain alternative contact methods (phone/iPhone as indicated)
- Confirm all specifications in writing despite email problems
