# GS1 UK Survey Feedback Request

## Summary
GS1 UK periodically sends survey requests to gather member feedback on their services and platforms (My GS1 and My Numberbank). These emails typically offer incentive prize draws (e.g., £250 John Lewis vouchers) and are conducted through independent research agencies like YouGov. The surveys aim to understand member priorities and improve service delivery. **Treat these as low-priority informational emails unless specifically requested by your client to participate.**

## Email Characteristics

### Sender Information
- **From:** GS1 UK Communications (comms.gs1uk.org domain)
- **Key Contact:** Anne Godfrey (CEO signature)
- **Support Email:** marketing@gs1uk.org

### Typical Content Elements
1. Survey deadline (typically 2-3 weeks from initial send)
2. Prize draw incentive details
3. Multiple call-to-action links to the same survey
4. "View in browser" option
5. Reference to independent research partner (YouGov)
6. Unsubscribe/preference management links

## Handling Instructions

### 1. Initial Assessment
- **Check client preferences:** Review Cairn notes for whether client participates in industry surveys
- **Priority level:** Generally low unless client has active GS1 UK membership queries
- **Filing:** Archive under "GS1 UK Communications" or equivalent category

### 2. Client Communication Decision Tree

**DO NOT forward to client if:**
- Client has previously declined industry survey participation
- No active GS1 UK service issues or renewal discussions
- Client uses managed service model (NBNE handles all admin)

**CONSIDER forwarding if:**
- Client is actively reviewing GS1 UK services or costs
- Client has recently raised concerns about GS1 UK platforms
- Client specifically requested to see all GS1 UK communications
- Survey relates to pricing/service changes that may affect renewals

**ALWAYS forward if:**
- Client has explicitly requested participation in feedback surveys
- Cairn notes indicate client is a GS1 UK stakeholder or committee member

### 3. Response Actions

If client wishes to participate:
1. Forward the email with survey links intact
2. Note the deadline in Cairn with a reminder 2-3 days before closure
3. Confirm if client wants NBNE to complete on their behalf (some surveys allow this)

If declining or ignoring:
1. File email in appropriate folder
2. No response required
3. Update Cairn if this represents a policy change

## Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **WARNING: Link Verification**
- GS1 UK emails contain numerous tracking links
- Always verify the comms.gs1uk.org domain before clicking
- Phishing emails may impersonate GS1 UK with similar branding
- Check sender email address carefully (not just display name)

⚠️ **Survey Timing Issues**
- Surveys often have tight deadlines (10-14 days)
- "Final chance" emails may arrive with <48 hours remaining
- Don't commit client to participation without confirming availability

⚠️ **Data Privacy Considerations**
- YouGov and similar agencies collect survey responses separately
- Client may need to review privacy policy before participating
- Some clients have policies against third-party market research
