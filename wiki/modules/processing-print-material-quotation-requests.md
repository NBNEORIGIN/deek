# Processing Print Material Quotation Requests

## Summary
This article covers how to handle quotation requests for promotional materials including foam banners, roller banners, and printed cards. The client (Amit Kapoor) has requested two separate quotes with different card quantities, requiring pricing information and delivery/collection timelines for an event or promotional campaign.

## Request Details

### Quote 1 - With Ishihara Cards
- **A1 5mm Foam Banners**: 2 units
- **Roller Banners (800mm × 2000mm)**: 2 units
- **A3 Roller Banners**: 2 units
- **A5 Printed Cards (250-300 GSM)**: 156 cards (includes thank you cards AND Ishihara test cards)

### Quote 2 - Without Ishihara Cards
- **A1 5mm Foam Banners**: 2 units
- **Roller Banners (800mm × 2000mm)**: 2 units
- **A3 Roller Banners**: 2 units
- **A5 Printed Cards (250-300 GSM)**: 110 cards (thank you cards only)

## Processing Steps

### 1. Clarify Specifications
Before sourcing quotes, confirm the following with the client:
- **Design files**: Are artwork files ready? What format (PDF, AI, PSD)?
- **Card stock preference**: Matte or gloss finish for the 250-300 GSM cards?
- **Ishihara cards**: Confirm these are colour blindness test cards requiring precise colour calibration
- **Banner content**: Are these single-sided or double-sided prints?
- **Roller banner hardware**: Does client need stands included or print-only?

### 2. Vendor Selection
Contact at least 2-3 print vendors for competitive quotes. Consider:
- Vendors with colour calibration certificates (essential for Ishihara cards)
- Local suppliers if collection is preferred over delivery
- Turnaround time capabilities (express vs. standard)

### 3. Specify Technical Requirements
When requesting vendor quotes, provide:
- Exact dimensions for each item
- Material specifications (5mm foam board, card stock weight)
- Colour requirements (CMYK, Pantone references if applicable)
- Finishing requirements (lamination, mounting)
- Quantity requirements for each quote option

### 4. Obtain Delivery Information
Request from vendors:
- Production timeline (design approval to completion)
- Delivery options and costs
- Collection availability and location
- Express service availability and surcharge

### 5. Prepare Quote Response
Format your response to include:
- Itemized pricing for Quote 1 and Quote 2
- Lead time for production
- Delivery cost breakdown (if applicable)
- Collection details (address, hours, contact)
- Quote validity period
- Payment terms

## Warnings and Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **Ishihara Card Color Accuracy**: These are medical diagnostic tools requiring precise color reproduction. Ensure the vendor:
- Uses calibrated monitors and printers
- Can provide color proofs before full production
- Has experience with medical/diagnostic printing

⚠️ **GSM Specification Range**: Client specified "250-300 GSM" - confirm exact weight preference to avoid reprints.

⚠️ **Roller Banner Dimensions**: The "A3 roller banners" specification is unusual. A3 paper size (297mm × 420mm) is not standard for roller banners. Verify if client means:
- Desktop/tabletop roller banners
- A3 portrait orientation scaled up
- A different size altogether

⚠️ **Design Approval**: Ensure design sign-off before production begins. Banner and card reprints are costly.

⚠️ **Delivery Timing**: Client asks "how soon" - this suggests urgency. Flag if standard turnaround won't meet potential deadlines.
