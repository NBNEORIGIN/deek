# Fascia Sign Installation — NBNE Standard Options and Process

## Overview

Fascia signs are fixed horizontal signs mounted to building frontages. NBNE offers several standard fascia sign configurations, typically ranging from £1,228 to £4,730 + VAT depending on materials, size, and lighting options. All quoted prices include design, fabrication, and installation using NBNE's own pop-up scaffold.

## Standard Fascia Sign Options

### Option A: Flexiface Panel

**Construction:**
- Printed PVC banner mounted in slim aluminium flexiface frame
- Standard size: 3000mm × 500mm (can be adjusted)
- Lightweight, clean appearance

**Pricing:**
- Base cost: £1,228 + VAT (including design, manufacture, and installation)

**Limitations:**
- Gold lettering **cannot be achieved** with this method
- Text must be printed in flat colour (warm yellow or bronze tone) to simulate gold effect
- Suitable for clients requiring lower cost and easy future replacement

**Benefits:**
- Quick to produce
- Easy to re-skin in future if banner needs replacement

### Option B: ACM Letters on Black ACM Board

**Construction:**
- CNC-cut 3mm metallic gold ACM lettering
- Mounted on black ACM board with 3m bullnose trim
- Standard size: 3000mm × ~430mm
- Provides real metallic finish

**Pricing:**
- Base cost: £1,710 + VAT (including design, manufacture, and installation)

**Benefits:**
- High-quality traditional fascia style
- Real metallic gold lettering (not printed)
- Excellent durability
- Strong visibility and smart finish

### Option 3: 3D Stand-Off Letters (Direct Mount)

**Construction:**
- 10mm thick acrylic letters with metallic gold face
- Approx. 5.2m wide
- Mounted directly to stone façade using 16mm snap-on standoffs
- Continuous 5.2m trough light mounted above

**Pricing:**
- £2,420 – £3,270 + VAT
- Includes design, fabrication, and installation of letters and light

### Option 4: 3D Stand-Off Letters on Backboard

**Construction:**
- Same lettering specification as Option 3
- Letters mounted to 5.5m × 0.58m ACM tray/backboard
- Backboard built from 3 panels within aluminium frame
- Continuous 5.5m trough light mounted above

**Pricing:**
- £3,180 – £4,730 + VAT
- Includes full tray system, fabrication, and installation

**Note:** Options 3 and 4 are significantly more expensive due to 10mm thick acrylic letters with metallic gold face and continuous trough lighting.

## Optional Add-On: Trough Lighting

**Standard Specification:**
- 3m aluminium troughlight
- Material cost: £255.67 + VAT and delivery
- Labour for fitting and alignment: approx. £120 + VAT
- Total installed cost: approx. £700–£800

**Important:**
- Provides even illumination across the fascia
- Electrical connection **not included** in standard pricing
- Can be arranged separately if needed

## Installation Process

### What's Included in All Fascia Quotes

1. Site measurement and final sizing
2. Design and layout preparation
3. Fabrication
4. All materials and fixings
5. Installation using NBNE's own pop-up scaffold (no external access hire required)
6. Artwork proofs for sign-off

### Standard Terms

- **Deposit:** 25% required to confirm order
- **Balance:** Due on completion
- **Production timeline:** Confirm with client after artwork sign-off

### Installation Method

- NBNE uses own pop-up scaffold for all fascia installations
- No external scaffold hire costs passed to client
- For stone façades: 16mm snap-on standoffs for direct mounting
- For backboard systems: ACM tray built from multiple panels within aluminium frame

## Pricing Factors

Key cost drivers for fascia signs:

1. **Material choice:**
   - Printed flexiface: lowest cost
   - 3mm ACM: mid-range
   - 10mm acrylic with metallic finish: highest cost

2. **Lighting:**
   - Trough light adds approximately £700–£800 installed
   - Electrical connection adds further cost if required

3. **Mounting system:**
   - Direct mount: lower cost
   - Backboard/tray system: adds £760–£1,460 to quote

4. **Size:**
   - Standard 3m fascias are most economical
   - Larger continuous installations (5.2m+) increase costs proportionally

## Sales Process Notes

### Initial Quotation Stage

- Provide visual mockups showing option on building façade
- Scale proportions to fit neatly above entrance
- Prepare preliminary visuals before final artwork and quotation

### Customer Communication

- Clearly explain material limitations (e.g., flexiface cannot achieve true gold lettering)
- Offer cost-down options if initial quotes exceed customer expectations
- Confirm invoicing details: business name, billing address, and email for accounts

### Measurement Requirements

- Site measurement required before finalizing design and bracket dimensions
- Particularly important for projecting signs to confirm viewing angle from street
