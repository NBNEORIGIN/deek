## Available Thicknesses and Colours

### Standard Thickness Availability

**Coloured acrylic** is only available in **3mm and 5mm** thicknesses from FFIS.

**10mm acrylic** is available only in:
- Black
- White  
- Clear

This limitation was confirmed in November 2025 correspondence regarding custom colour requests.

### Clear Acrylic

Available in multiple thicknesses including:
- 3mm
- 5mm
- 8mm (confirmed available for custom cut panels 620mm x 35mm)
- 10mm

### Opal/Translucent Acrylic

**3mm opal acrylic** is available for backlit tray applications. NBNE has ordered panels up to 970mm x 1270mm in this specification.

### Standard Sheet Sizes

- Standard Perspex sheet size: **3050mm x 2030mm** (confirmed for lime green acrylic, March 2025)
- 10mm white acrylic: **2m x 1m** sheets quoted

## Colour Availability and Limitations

### Special/Custom Colours

**Custom RAL colours in 10mm are NOT readily available**. Special colours require large minimum order quantities (MOQ) of sheets, making them impractical for typical NBNE order sizes.

When requesting custom colours (e.g. RGB: 132,198,100 mentioned in November 2025), FFIS advises selecting from standard Perspex colour range only.

### Standard Colours Available

FFIS stocks standard Perspex colours including:
- **Lime green** (standard lime green or M7-160) — available in 3mm and 5mm, no MOQ
- **Cream** — described as "a lot creamier" than some reference samples
- **Brown** — available but colour selection required from stock options
- Various standard Perspex range colours (colour chart available from FFIS)

### Colour Matching Notes

When colour matching is critical:
- FFIS can provide samples before ordering
- Physical colour checking at FFIS premises is possible — arrange with Lyndsey or Amanda
- Example: February 2026, Toby visited FFIS to check cream ACM colour against sample

## Ordering Process

### Standard Ordering

1. Contact Lyndsey Gray (or Amanda Vasey if Lyndsey unavailable) via email
2. Specify:
   - Material type (acrylic)
   - Thickness
   - Colour
   - Dimensions required
3. FFIS provides quotation, typically with stock confirmation
4. Material often in stock for standard specifications

### Custom Cut Panels

FFIS can supply **custom cut panels** rather than full sheets. Examples:
- 4 panels @ 620mm x 35mm in 8mm clear acrylic
- 3 panels @ 313mm x 802mm in 5mm clear acrylic  
- 970mm x 1270mm in 3mm opal

This reduces waste and cost for specific job requirements.

### Short Notice/Emergency Orders

FFIS can accommodate short-notice orders when material is in stock. Example: February 2026 order for cream ACM to "replace a cock-up we made for a sign tray" — same-day quotation provided.

## Best Practices

1. **Always specify exact dimensions** to get accurate quotes and minimize waste
2. **Request custom cut panels** for non-standard sizes rather than buying full sheets
3. **Check colour availability early** in job planning — custom colours in thick material require impractical MOQs
4. **For colour-critical jobs**, arrange physical sample checking or visit FFIS
5. **Use Lyndsey Gray as primary contact**; Amanda Vasey as backup
6. **Confirm stock availability** when jobs have tight deadlines (though FFIS typically stocks standard items)

## Known Limitations

- No 4mm thickness in coloured acrylic (only 3mm and 5mm)
- 10mm limited to black/white/clear only
- Custom RAL colours impractical due to MOQ requirements
- Cream acrylic noted as "creamier" than some expectations — check samples
