# Aluminium Composite Panel (ACM) Suppliers and Procurement

## Overview

NBNE uses aluminium composite material (ACM) for a variety of signage applications, including exterior fascia boards, directory panels, and substrate for direct UV printing. This article documents our current suppliers, standard specifications, and procurement considerations.

## Current ACM Specifications

**Standard material used:**
- **3mm ACM panels** – most common substrate for signage
- **Skybond Hoarding ACM White Matt/Primer** – typical specification for hoarding and general signage work
- Standard sheet size: 2440 × 1220 mm
- Skin thickness: 0.15mm (standard for hoarding-grade ACM)

**Common applications:**
- CNC-cut lettering and shapes
- Direct flatbed UV printing (onto gloss white ACM)
- External fascia boards with cut vinyl or printed graphics
- Directory and wayfinding panels

## ACM vs Foamex – Customer Guidance

NBNE typically recommends ACM over Foamex (PVC foam board) for the following reasons:

**Advantages of ACM:**
- Superior durability, especially for outdoor use
- Better resistance to impact and handling damage
- Recommended for long-term or high-traffic applications
- More professional appearance

**When Foamex may be acceptable:**
- Short-term indoor applications
- Budget-constrained projects where durability is less critical

**Comparative pricing (small format):**
- Foamex (3mm) A4: £13.50 + VAT vs ACM A4: £17.50 + VAT
- Foamex (3mm) A3: £24.00 + VAT vs ACM A3: £31.00 + VAT

The price premium for ACM is approximately 30%, which should be communicated to customers as better value for long-term use.

## Alternative Materials Considered

NBNE has explored **sublimation aluminium** (solid aluminium sheet) as a potential replacement for 3mm gloss white ACM for CNC cutting and flatbed UV printing applications.

**Consideration:** Thicker sublimation aluminium (1.5mm or 2mm) as ACM replacement
- **Supplier investigated:** Worthwill (cici@worthwillal.com) – direct manufacturer in Zhengzhou, China
- Custom sizes and finishes available
- **MOQ issue:** 3 tons per colour/size with 35-40 day lead time
- **Outcome:** Not viable for NBNE due to high MOQ requirements

Stock items were available in limited sizes but better suited to small-format sublimation work rather than ACM replacement.

**Recommendation:** Continue using 3mm ACM from Amari Plastics for CNC/UV applications rather than switching to solid aluminium.

## Procurement Best Practices

1. **Standard specification:** Maintain 3mm white ACM (2440 × 1220mm) as standard stock item
2. **Bulk ordering:** Evaluate volume discounts with Amari – current pricing suggests different SKUs may have quantity breaks
3. **Alternative finishes:** Request stock list from Amari for coloured, brushed, and mirror ACM when quoting specialist projects
4. **Lead times:** Typical turnaround 2-3 working days from artwork approval (includes ACM procurement and processing)
5. **Verify specifications:** Confirm skin thickness and core material with supplier – hoarding-grade (0.15mm skin) may not be suitable for all applications
