## Standard Stock Items and Availability

### Thickness Options

**Coloured Acrylic (Perspex brand):**
- Available in **3mm and 5mm only** (confirmed June 2024 and November 2025)
- 10mm coloured acrylic only available in black, white, or clear
- Special colours in 10mm require large minimum order quantities (impractical for typical NBNE projects)

**Clear Acrylic:**
- Available in multiple thicknesses including 3mm, 5mm, 8mm, 10mm
- No minimum order restrictions

### Sheet Sizes

- Standard full sheet: **3050mm x 2030mm** (confirmed for coloured acrylic)
- 10mm white acrylic: **2m x 1m** sheets available

### Standard Colours in Stock

FFIS stocks standard Perspex colours with **no minimum order quantity** for 3mm and 5mm thicknesses. Colours mentioned as available:
- White (multiple thicknesses)
- Black
- Clear
- Opal (for backlit applications)
- Lime green (closest match to M7-160 available in 3mm and 5mm)
- Cream (note: FFIS cream is "a lot creamier" than some reference samples)
- Brown (available but colour options need to be specified)

## Custom Sizing and Cut Panels

FFIS can supply custom-cut panels rather than full sheets. Examples from orders:
- 4 panels of 8mm clear acrylic at 620mm x 35mm
- 3 panels at 313mm x 802mm in 5mm clear
- Single panel 970mm x 1270mm in 3mm opal

No minimum order quantity appears to apply to cut panels.

## Custom Colour Matching

**RAL and RGB colour matching is problematic:**
- Example: RGB 132,198,100 requested in 10mm thickness (November 2025)
- Custom colours in 10mm require large minimum orders
- Best approach: work with standard Perspex colour range or consider alternative methods (vinyl wrap, paint, etc.)

## Lead Times

FFIS maintains good stock levels:
- Standard items often quoted as "in stock" 
- Quick turnaround possible for urgent orders (e.g., same-day quotation for rush cream ACM panel)

## Processing Notes

### Laser Cutting

## Ordering Process

Standard practice from emails:
1. Contact Lyndsey Gray or Amanda Vasey with specifications
2. Receive quotation (typically attached PDF)
3. Confirm order via email
4. If contacts are out of office, emails auto-forward to sales@ffis.co.uk

**Tip:** When ordering, specify:
- Thickness
- Colour/finish
- Dimensions (full sheet or cut panels)
- Quantity
- Any urgency/delivery requirements

## Key Lessons

1. **Don't assume 10mm coloured is available** - always check. Only black, white, clear stocked in this thickness.

2. **Cream ACM colour varies** - request sample or swatch when colour match is critical.

3. **FFIS is responsive** - good for rush orders and same-day quotes.

4. **No minimum orders** - unlike some suppliers, FFIS will supply small quantities of standard colours.

5. **Custom RGB/RAL matching impractical** - work within standard Perspex range or use alternative finishing methods.
