## What is Sign Recovery?

Sign recovery involves applying new vinyl material over an existing sign substrate rather than manufacturing a completely new sign. This is a cost-effective solution when the underlying sign structure is still sound but the face material is worn, faded, or damaged.

## Key Requirements

1. **Material**: Matte vinyl finish (as specifically requested by customer)
2. **Scope**: Recovery only - no new sign manufacture required
3. **Installation**: To be confirmed whether NBNE is handling installation or customer will arrange

## Process Steps

### 1. Initial Assessment
- Confirm the existing sign substrate is suitable for recovery
- Check for structural damage, warping, or corrosion
- Measure exact dimensions of the sign face
- Photograph current condition for records

### 2. Material Selection
- Customer specified matte vinyl
- Ensure matte vinyl is suitable for the sign's location (indoor vs outdoor)
- Consider durability requirements based on exposure

### 3. Design & Proofing
- Obtain existing artwork or recreate design
- Prepare vinyl artwork to exact sign dimensions
- Send proof to customer for approval before production
- Confirm colours match customer expectations (matte finishes can appear different)

### 4. Production
- Print matte vinyl to specifications
- Include bleed allowance for wrapping edges if applicable
- Perform quality check on print before dispatch

### 5. Installation
- **Clarify with customer**: Who is handling installation?
- If NBNE installing: Schedule site visit and confirm access requirements
- If customer installing: Provide application instructions

## Warnings & Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **Email Communication Issues**: The original correspondence mentions "problems with your email address" - ensure you have reliable contact details before proceeding. Always confirm receipt of critical information.

⚠️ **Matte Vinyl Considerations**:
- Matte finishes show fingerprints and handling marks more easily during installation
- Harder to clean than gloss finishes once installed
- May not be suitable for high-traffic areas or locations requiring frequent cleaning
- Ensure customer understands maintenance requirements

⚠️ **Surface Preparation**: The existing sign face must be thoroughly cleaned and degreased. Any contaminants will cause adhesion failure.

⚠️ **Substrate Condition**: Always inspect the existing sign before quoting. Hidden damage may mean recovery isn't viable and a new sign is required.

## Best Practices

- **Document Everything**: Take before/during/after photos for your records and customer satisfaction
- **Confirm Scope**: Be explicit about what is/isn't included (removal, installation, disposal of old materials)
- **Contact Details**: Verify email and phone contacts early in the project, especially if previous communication issues occurred
- **Material Samples**: Consider providing a matte vinyl sample for customer approval if this is their first matte finish sign

## Pricing Considerations

Sign recovery is typically charged based on:
- Square footage of vinyl required
- Complexity of design/application
- Site visit costs (if applicable)
- Installation labour (if in scope)
- Any surface preparation needed

Recovery should be significantly cheaper than new sign manufacture since you're reusing the existing substrate.
