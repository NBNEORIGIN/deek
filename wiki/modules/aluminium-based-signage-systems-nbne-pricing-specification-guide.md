# Aluminium-Based Signage Systems — NBNE Pricing & Specification Guide

## Overview

This article consolidates NBNE's standard pricing and technical specifications for aluminium-based signage systems, drawn from recent quotations (2025). It covers ACM (aluminium composite) panels, CNC-cut aluminium components, aluminium tray signs, and associated illumination options.

---

## ACM Panel Systems

### Vinyl Graphics on ACM (Flat Panel)

**Typical specification:**
- 3 mm ACM panels (standard size: 8 × 4 ft / 2440 × 1220 mm)
- Cut or printed vinyl lettering, UV over-laminated
- Direct wall-fix or frame-mounted

**Pricing (2025):**
- **Two 8 × 4 ft panels with vinyl graphics:** £575–£650 (ex. VAT)
- **Simple 8 × 2 ft printed ACM board (supply only):** £120 + VAT
- **Lead time:** ±1 week from artwork approval and 50% deposit

**Notes:**
- Most cost-effective option for fascia refresh
- Clean, flat finish; suitable for long-term exterior use
- Cannot achieve true metallic gold; printed "gold effect" uses warm yellow or bronze tone

---

### CNC-Cut ACM Lettering on ACM Tray

**Typical specification:**
- 3 mm metallic gold ACM letters, CNC-cut
- Mounted on colour-matched ACM backboard (e.g. black) with bullnose trim
- Example dimensions: 3000 × 500 mm fascia

**Pricing (2025):**
- **3 mm gold ACM letters on black ACM board with 3 m bullnose trim:** £1,710 + VAT (inc. design, manufacture, installation)
- **Lead time:** ±1½ weeks from artwork approval

**Notes:**
- Offers genuine metallic finish vs. printed gold effect
- High durability; traditional fascia appearance
- Significantly more expensive than flat vinyl graphics due to CNC machining and material cost

---

## Flexiface Banner Systems

**Typical specification:**
- Slim aluminium frame with tensioned printed PVC banner skin
- Re-skinnable for future updates

**Pricing (2025):**
- **3000 × 500 mm flexiface panel (inc. design, manufacture, installation):** £1,228 + VAT
- **Generic guide (larger fascia areas, supply only):** £600–£800 + VAT

**Notes:**
- Lightweight, clean appearance, quick to produce
- Gold lettering cannot be achieved — printed warm yellow/bronze tone only
- Popular for larger fascia areas where re-branding may occur

---

## Aluminium Tray Signs

**Typical specification:**
- Folded aluminium tray (approx. 8 × 2 ft standard)
- Printed or cut vinyl graphics applied to face

**Pricing (2025):**
- **Aluminium tray with printed graphics (supply only):** £800–£1,000 + VAT
- **Tray with 3D stand-off or acrylic letters (supply only):** £1,300–£1,800 + VAT

**Notes:**
- Creates clean, dimensional fascia
- Premium appearance; suitable for high-visibility or branding-sensitive sites
- 3D letter option adds depth and perceived value

---

## Framed ACM Systems

**Typical specification:**
- Printed ACM panel mounted within powder-coated aluminium frame (e.g. **Tradesignz** system)
- Neater, more professional finish than bare ACM board

**Pricing (2025):**
- **Framed ACM board (supply only):** £350–£450 + VAT (depending on colour and size)

**Notes:**
- Mid-range option between flat board and full tray system
- Frame colour and size affect final price

---

## Built-Up 3D Letters (Individual-Fixed)

**Typical specification:**
- 30–60 mm depth, trade-supplied moulded or fabricated letters
- Powder-coated finish
- Optional halo or face-lighting

**Pricing (2025):**
- **Non-illuminated built-up letters (individually fixed):** £1,250–£1,450
- **Add halo LED lighting:** +£300–£400
- **Lead time:** 2–3 weeks (supplier lead time applies)

**Notes:**
- Maximum visual impact and longevity
- Halo lighting provides evening presence
- Supplier lead time must be factored into project schedule

---

## Acrylic Letter Systems

### Flat-Cut Acrylic on ACM Tray

**Typical specification:**
- 10 mm CNC-cut acrylic letters (painted or brushed-aluminium-faced)
- Bonded to colour-matched ACM tray panel

**Pricing (2025):**
- **10 mm flat-cut acrylic on ACM tray:** £900–£1,050
- **Lead time:** ±1½ weeks

**Notes:**
- Mid-range statement option
- 10 mm depth adds crisp shadow line and "built" appearance without full cost of 3D built-ups

### 10 mm Thick Acrylic Letters (Metallic Gold Face)

**Notes from recent estimates:**
- Metallic gold-faced acrylic letters contribute significantly to material and fabrication cost
- Used in combination with ACM tray/backboard systems and trough lighting, total estimates range £3,180–£4,730 (ex. VAT) for 5.5 m fascia installations including design, fabrication, and installation

## Illumination Options

### Aluminium Trough Lights

**Standard specification:**
- 3 m LED trough light unit
- Mounted above or below fascia for even illumination

**Pricing (2025):**
- **3 m aluminium trough light (supply only):** £255.67 + VAT + delivery
- **Alternative supply-only guide price:** ~£450 + VAT
- **Labour for fitting and alignment:** ~£120 + VAT

**Notes:**
- **Contradiction flagged:** Two different supply-only prices quoted (£255.67 vs. £450). Likely reflects different supplier or specification; confirm with purchasing before quoting.
- Once supplied and installed, typical total cost contribution: £700–£800

## CNC-Machined Aluminium Components (Small-Run)

**Example specification (custom aluminium signs):**
- Quantity: 12
- Material: 2 mm aluminium
- Size: 80 × 28 mm
- Finish: UV-printed vinyl with protective overlaminate
- Fixing: VHB tape applied to rear
- CNC-cut for precise edges

**Pricing (2025):**
- **Unit price (qty 12):** £8.15/unit
- **Total (ex. VAT):** £97.80 (£117.36 inc. VAT)
- **Lead time:** 5–7 working days from artwork approval

**Notes:**
- Small-run CNC-machined metal parts are costly due to setup and machining time
- Includes all materials, CNC machining, printing, lamination, VHB tape application, and QC

## Installation & Project Management

**Standard inclusions (for full-service quotes):**
- Design and layout preparation
- Artwork proof for client approval prior to production
- Manufacture
- Installation using NBNE's own pop-up scaffold (no external access hire required for standard fascia work)
- Electrical connection (if illuminated; typically by client's electrician or sub-contracted)

**Deposit & payment terms:**
- **Standard deposit:** 50% on approval of artwork (for most signage projects)
- **Alternative (smaller jobs):** 25% deposit, balance due prior to collection/dispatch
- Lead times begin from deposit receipt and artwork approval
