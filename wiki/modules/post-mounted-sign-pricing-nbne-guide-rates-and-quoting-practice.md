# Post-Mounted Sign Pricing — NBNE Guide Rates and Quoting Practice

## Overview

Post-mounted signs (freestanding entrance signs, directional totems, site directories, etc.) are a recurring product category. This article consolidates pricing benchmarks, specification norms, and quoting practices drawn from real NBNE client correspondence.

---

## Standard Configurations & Price Benchmarks

### Freestanding Entrance Signs (New Posts)

**Typical specification:**
- 1.2 m × 0.8 m (or 1830 mm × 610 mm / 6′ × 2′) ACM panel
- Aluminium posts (square or round section)
- Full-colour print with overlaminate, or cut vinyl
- Ground-mounted posts (client or NBNE installation)

**Guide pricing (supply only, posts included):**
- **£800–£1,200 + VAT** (standard range)
- Examples from client quotes:
  - Middleton Estates (Nov 2025): *"1.2 m × 0.8 m ACM panel between aluminium posts… £800–£1,200 + VAT"*
  - Price assumes new post fabrication/supply

**Important qualifier:**  
Multiple emails note: *"if the posts are already in-situ the price will be significantly lower"*. When posts exist, price the **panel only** (see below) plus modest fixings/labour uplift.

## Quoting Process & Client Communication

### Lead Time

- **Standard panels (printed ACM):** 5–7 working days from artwork sign-off (Aln Valley Railway, multiple instances)
- **Installation scheduling:** 5–7 working days following manufacture, weather permitting

### Information to Confirm Before Finalising Quote

Standard checklist (drawn from Toby's repeat requests to Aln Valley Railway & Middleton Estates):

1. **Final dimensions** — does the stated size work, or adjustment needed?
2. **Mounting method** — rail & posts, pre-drilled holes, or supply-only?
3. **Installation requirement** — NBNE install, client install, or third-party?
4. **Artwork status** — client-supplied or NBNE design required?

### Artwork & Proofs

- **Artwork/proofs line item:** £75 (Aln Valley Railway, Aug 2025: *"Artwork & proofs (master template) – £75.00"*)
- Applies when NBNE creates template or substantial layout work

## Price Variance & Flexibility Notes

### Size Tolerance

Multiple Aln Valley Railway quotes include:  
*"Price holds within ±10 mm if you prefer a slight size tweak"*

This provides flexibility for clients matching existing fixings without re-quoting.

### Material Yield Efficiency

The 930 × 230 mm totem dimension was explicitly chosen because it *"works well for sheet yield"*. When quoting custom sizes, check material waste impact before committing to fixed pricing.

### "Supply Only" vs. Full Service

Middleton Estates quotes consistently state *"supply only (you mentioned you'll be fitting these yourself)"* — this is a significant cost reduction vs. turnkey pricing. Always clarify scope in initial email.

## Contradictions / Points for Review

1. **Entrance sign price range variation:**  
   The £800–£1,200 bracket appears stable across 2025 quotes, but no detailed breakdown is provided for *what drives the £400 variance*. Likely factors: post height, finish (powder coat colour), panel complexity, but this should be documented in future quotes.

2. **Small-format pricing gaps:**  
   The A4 benchmark (£17.50) is clear, but intermediate sizes (e.g., 600 × 400 mm) lack documented pricing. Board Advisory Partners enquiry went unanswered in excerpts provided — may be worth establishing tiered pricing table for common staked-sign sizes.

3. **Installation pricing inconsistency:**  
   £45 for single-sign Alnmouth job vs. £1,000 full-day team rate suggests need for clearer day-rate / per-sign installation pricing structure.
